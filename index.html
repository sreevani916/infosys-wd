FAQ: New Employees

  • Basics

  • When should I arrive?

    You can check your joining ate on the given offer letter. Please arrive on morning at 9:00 AM for continuing the on-boarding process.

  • Where do I go when I arrive?

    You can check your office location address on the given offer letter.

  • Can I request extra training?

    Yes you can request for extra training by showing your manager why your business needs to upskill now and demonstrate the tangible results that training will bring.

    And finally explain how you will move forward after the training course.

  • Who will I be reporting to?

    You received an email on the date of joining from your HR partner. You can find the details in that mail.

    If you need any details on this, please reach out to your HR Partner.

  • What are my main objectives for week one?

    New hire checklist template for the first week.

    1. Open the Control Send a series of welcome emails to your new hire.

    2. Have new hires fill out digitized paperwork

    3. In the new Set up a workstation with necessary tools, devices and access.

    4. Make sure IT sets up all necessary accounts and logins.

  • How can I get entry in office building?

    Your Manager will raise the building access request through the Access@ABC-ISA tool. Upon approval, a system notification will be sent to the employee informing the RTW dates and the work location. As per statutory requirement, employees will have to do a self-declaration in the ABC Site Access tool everyday one hour before getting into the office.

  • Who do I call if I’m running late?

    You need to call your manager or mentor if you are running late. You can also inform them by mail or message.

  • Is there on-site parking?

    Parking is available in the basement of the building. You require a parking pass which you get from reception.

  • What should I wear?

    We are following open culture about dressing up. You can wear formal or informal clothes on office premises.

  • What will my first day involve?

    You must come to the office at 9:00 PM on the first day. We will schedule several meetings with you to complete the onboarding process. In the afternoon, the office will arrange lunch for newcomers. After lunch, you will get allocated your desk.

  • Can I start training before my first day?

    Training must be done during paid work hours only. Full access to the employee portal will not be granted until your start date. Also, visit www.ABC.com/system-related/training-details/ - for more details on trainings.

  • Should I bring lunch or do people eat out?

    You can carry your tiffin with you. For keeping the office environment clean, We do not allow to eat tiffin on the desk. You can use the canteen area to eat food. Also in the office canteen area, veg and non-veg options are available.

  • When and how do I get paid?

    Salary is credited on the last working day of the month for all regular employees.

  • What expenses am I allowed and how do I claim?

    If you have to travel for an office meeting, Office will cover travelling and lunch-dinner expenses. You need to claim expenses on the office portal. Use the original bill to get a claim.

  • How do I request a holiday?

    1. Access View Holiday Calendar

    2. In the Holiday Calendar dropdown field, select your respective state (eg: Bangalore, Pune, Chennai, etc.)

    3. Select OK.

    4. You can find the list of holidays for the past year from when Workday has gone live until the current year.

    5. You can use the Excel icon on the top right corner or the table to download the holiday list. Also, visit www.ABC.com/system-related/holiday-request/ -for more information on holiday request."

  • How does holiday work?

  • How much notice should I give to book holiday?

    If you want to take planned leave, You require to inform your manager or mentor before 1 week. If you want to apply for sick leave, You can inform the manager on the same day of leave.

  • What’s the process if I’m sick?

    You don't need to apply for sick leave on the same day. Once you will get recovered, You can apply for sick leave for that day.

  • Can I work from home occasionally?

    Work from home is allowed for 2 days a week. If you are not able to come to the office you need to inform the reason to your manager and mentor. Please visit the page www.abc.work-from-home/details - to know more about when to take work from home.

  • If I lost any company asset, whom should I reach out?

    In IT Asset Management, when an asset is reported to be lost or stolen, the first step is to file an official police report in the country where the incident took place.
